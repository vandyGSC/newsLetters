Registration will open starting early 2017 and has started recruiting volunteers. Please contact gsc_academic@vanderbilt.edu if you are interested in helping plan or volunteer the day of the event.
Ombudsman

GSC will be preparing a proposal for the hiring of a graduate student Ombudsman to submit to the Graduate School. Stay tuned for updates.

$300,000,000 endowment

Chancellor Zeppos has allocated a $300 million dollar endowment to Graduate Research and Education as part of his vision for inclusive excellence. GSC has been given the opportunity to organize proposals for the use of these funds. We plan to send an email out to the entire graduate community with more details soon.

Life Coach

The University hopes to hire a Life Coach for graduate students within the next few months! The role of the Life Coach will probably overlap with the career center and PCC
